PAKISTAN


Country: Islamic Republic of Pakistan

Capital: Islamabad

Official Languages: Urdu (national language), English (official language for government and business)

Population: Over 225 million people (as of 2021)

Currency: Pakistani Rupee (PKR)

Pakistan is located in South Asia and shares borders with India, Afghanistan, Iran, and China.

The country has diverse landscapes, including mountains (such as the Himalayas and Karakoram ranges), fertile plains, deserts, and a long coastline along the Arabian Sea.

Pakistan gained independence from British rule on August 14, 1947, when it separated from Hindustan to form a separate Muslim-majority nation.

The culture of Pakistan is a blend of various regional influences, including the ancient Indus Valley Civilization, Islamic traditions, and Persian, Central Asian, and British colonial legacies.

Pakistan has a rich cultural heritage, including traditional music (such as Qawwali), dance (such as Bhangra), and a diverse cuisine.

The historical city of Lahore, with landmarks like the Badshahi Mosque, Lahore Fort, and Walled City.

The ancient archaeological site of Mohenjo-daro, a UNESCO World Heritage site, dating back to the Indus Valley Civilization.

The stunning mountain ranges, including K2 (the world's second-highest peak) and the Karakoram Highway, a scenic road connecting Pakistan to China.

The beautiful valleys of Swat, Hunza, and Neelum, known for their natural beauty and hospitality.

The architectural wonders of Islamabad, the modern capital city, including Faisal Mosque, Pakistan Monument, and Rawal Lake.

Pakistan's economy is the 24th largest in the world by purchasing power parity.

Major industries include textiles, agriculture, manufacturing, and services.

Pakistan is also known for its agricultural products, including cotton, wheat, rice, and fruits.

Cricket is the most popular sport in Pakistan, and the national cricket team has achieved international success, winning the ICC Cricket World Cup in 1992.

Field hockey is also popular, with Pakistan winning multiple Olympic and World Cup titles in the past.
